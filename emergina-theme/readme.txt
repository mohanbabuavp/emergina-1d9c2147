Emergina — one-page black & white theme for digital marketing, impersonation removal and anti-piracy brands.

== Installation ==
1. Upload the emergina folder to /wp-content/themes/
2. Activate the theme in Appearance → Themes
3. Go to Settings → Reading → set "Your homepage displays" to "A static page" and pick any page (front-page.php will render automatically)
4. Update your business info under Appearance → Customize → Business Info
